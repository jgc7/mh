Video Assignment for Multimedia/Hypermedia Class

For this assignment, I decided to highlight my family's Myrtle Beach vacations. We go to Myrtle Beach every summer and always have a lot of fun. I used iMove to create this video. I did a lot of cropping to make the pictures fit the Ken Burns style, and also created several titles. I also adjusted the amount of seconds for some of the pictures to be shown. Hope you enjoy!
